About Starfind

Hello visitors! My name is Teryn and I am the creator of this site. I created Starfind as a school project for my Software Development class. I have always been enthralled with space and its many mysteries so I figured I would theme my site around my curiosity and intrigue. I hope you enjoy it and thanks for visiting!

Starfind FAQs

What is Starfind?

Starfind is a website that allows user to search for and buy various stars found on the starmap. Each star is based on an existing known star, however their locations are not accurate.

Why Starfind?

Starfind is an interactive experience that brings information about known stars and combines that into a game like simulation. The whole point is to learn and have fun!

What led to the idea behind Starfind?

My love for space and games!
